Geographical Segmentation in Telecommunication Services: Economic and Technological Aspects

Go to article

Import to BibTex

Authors

Álvaro Escribano, Arturo Azcorra, Enrique Cañizares, Alex Bikfalvi

Abstract

The EC and the different NRAs are studying the geographical scope of the markets included in the EC Recommendation. That is the case of Ofcom in UK, where the obligations imposed on the SMP Operator have been relieved in particular areas of the country. The article presents different studies which relate regulation, welfare and investment which pave the way to market segmentation.

Journal

Regulatory and Economic Policy in Telecommunications by Telefónica Spain
Volume 1, Issue 1, Pages 74-87, September 2008
